Required Documents

Choose your seller type to see what you'll need.

Company / Business Requirements
✅
Business Owner's ID Card
A clear copy of the owner's national ID
✅
Valid Commercial Registration
Your official business registration document
✅
Valid Tax Card
Shows you're registered to do business in Egypt
✅
Business Bank Account + IBAN & Stamped Bank Certificate
Account must be in the business name. Certificate must include IBAN and account number with bank stamp.
Individual Seller Requirements
✅
National ID Card
A clear copy of your national ID
✅
Tax Card
Your personal tax registration card
✅
Personal Bank Account + IBAN & Stamped Bank Certificate
To receive your payouts securely. Certificate must include IBAN and account number with bank stamp.
